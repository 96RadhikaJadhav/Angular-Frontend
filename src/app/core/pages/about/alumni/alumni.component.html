Alumni Network

At Confluente, we highly appreciate our Honors alumni. Are you a Confluente alumnus and do you want to stay connected with the association? Awesome! Confluente organizes special alumni events throughout the year, such as an alumni drink. Besides, some regular events are also open for alumni. You can join the alumni newsletter by sending an email to alumni@hsaconfluente.nl, or you can follow the Confluente LinkedIn page to see what Confluente is up to.

LinkedIn

The LinkedIn page of Confluente is the place for us to share important events, accomplishments and other interesting developments. It's also the place where we will introduce the new board every year, so alumni will have a better sense of what's happening and, more importantly, who it is they will most likely communicate with when there is interest to attend one of our events.

Alumni Newsletter

The Alumni Newsletter is a two-yearly email newsletter. It covers all alumni events as well as the latest developments within the association and the Honors Academy. Interested? Send an email to alumni@hsaconfluente.nl
